BMR is how many calories you burn when your body is resting.


Man BMR = 66 + (13.7 x weight) + (5 x height) - (6.8 x age)


Woman BMR = 665 + (9.6 x weight) + (1.8 x height) - (4.7 x age)



(Total  Daily  Energy  Expenditure) - T.D.E.E.


TDEE is an estimation of how many calories you burn each day , including physical activity.


TDEE = BMR x Exercise frequency (1.2 , 1.4 , 1.6 , 1.8 , 2.0)
